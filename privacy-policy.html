Privacy Policy

Devy: Learn to Code · Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Devy: Learn to Code ("Devy", "the App") handles information when you use the iOS/iPadOS application.

Information we collect

  • Sign in with Apple: If you create an account, Apple may share a stable user identifier and, on first sign-in only, your name and email address. We store your chosen display name and learning preferences on your device.
  • Learning data: Progress, streaks, gems, project files, and settings are stored locally on your device using SwiftData.
  • iCloud Key-Value Storage: If you are signed in, purchased project IDs and device profile hints may sync across your devices tied to the same Apple ID via Apple's iCloud KVS. We do not operate our own servers for this data.
  • Profile photo: Optional photos you choose are saved only on your device.
  • Purchases: Subscriptions and in-app purchases are processed by Apple. We receive entitlement status through StoreKit; we do not receive your payment card details.
  • Notifications: If enabled, the App schedules local study reminders on your device. We do not send push messages from external servers.

Subscriptions (Devy Premium)

Devy Premium is an auto-renewable subscription (monthly or yearly) billed through your Apple ID. Apple processes payment and provides us only with subscription status. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your device.

How we use information

We use the information above to provide the learning experience, sync purchases across your devices, personalize reminders, and restore your account when you sign in again.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

Data retention and deletion

Your data remains on your device and in your iCloud KVS until you delete it. You can delete your account at any time in the App: Settings tab → Account → Delete account. Account deletion permanently removes your Devy account record, local learning data, profile photo, and synced purchase metadata associated with your Apple ID in the App.

Deleting the App from your device removes local data. Active subscriptions must be cancelled separately in your Apple ID subscription settings.

Children

Devy is intended for learners aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to the developer through App Store Connect support for Devy: Learn to Code.
